Site Name

Southeast Idaho Radar. The more you know, the better prepared you are. I.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide up to the minute weather for the cities in and around Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven Idaho. There will be storm alerts n the event of severe weater. There will be community events listed and a way for users to share information and images regarding these events.
