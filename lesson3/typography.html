Typography

Headings

I have chosen Lobster for my h1 and h3 headings. It is a chunky, fun font that shows up well. I am using the complimenting font Montserrat as the h2 and h4 headings. I think a little Lobster goes a long way and I don't like two Lobster headings stacked.

Body

Montserrat will be a solid body font paired with Lobster. It has varying looks with each weight. I can use the lighter weights here and heavier in the headings.
